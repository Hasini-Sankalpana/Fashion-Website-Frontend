Trends

Stay Ahead, Embrace Fashion's Latest Vibes.

Micro-Fringe

Micro-Fringe is a trend that involves ultra-fine strands of fringe accenting hemlines and key elements of outerwear, dresses, and separates.

Designers like Bottega Veneta, Burberry, Gucci, Mugler, and Tom Ford have showcased this trend on the Spring/Summer 2024 runways. The size and refinement of 2024’s micro-fringe make it more subversive than previous, thicker variations.

Business Sensual

Business Sensual is a trend that has seen a drastic evolution in 20242. Throughout Fashion Month, gauzy sheer fabrics left the realm of ethereal gowns and entered the business casual chat. Designers like Sportmax, Givenchy, Philosophy By Lorenzo Serafini, and Issey Miyake sent blouses, midi skirts, and button-downs down the runway that wouldn’t look out of place in an office setting were they just a shade less sheer.
